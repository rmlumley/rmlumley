Rob and Caroline's Wisconsin Bookstore Tour

You + Me + Books and a weekend roadtrip across Wisconsin

Friday

Stevens Point

We drive 90 mins up to Stevens Point and go to Sojourn Bookstore and grab lunch.

Wausau

We then drive 40 minutes up to Wausau and visit Janke Bookstore, the oldest bookstore in Wisconsin. We get dinner and stay the night in Wausau.

Saturday

Rhinelander

We drive 60 minutes up to Rhinelander and go to Briar House for coffee and books.

Bayfield

We take a longer 2.5 hour drive and arrive in Bayfield and visit Honest Dog Books. We stay the night on Lake Superior.

Sunday

The long drive back

We wake up early, grab some coffee and make our return drive to Madison. It's a long day but we'll have each other and a car full of books.
